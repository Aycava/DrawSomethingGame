Tutorial

Welcome to Draw Something, a drawing game focused on getting you to draw something. To start, press the big play button on the home screen. Then input the amount of time you want to spend per drawing, and the amount of drawings you want to do....

About

Draw Something was specifically created for artists or aspiring artists who find trouble practicing their drawing skills due to a lack of ideas. It is intended to be used like a daily exercise to warm up your drawing "muscles"....

About

Draw Something was specifically created for artists or aspiring artists who find trouble practicing their drawing skills due to a lack of ideas. It is intended to be used like a daily exercise to warm up your drawing "muscles".
I really hope that someone is able to get some use out of this, as I do think the problem this game seeks to solve is more common than one might think. You could easily compare it to writers block.
But regardless of whether or not you enjoy my game, thank you for playing it and giving it a chance.

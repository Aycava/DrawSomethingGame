Tutorial

Welcome to Draw Something, a drawing game focused on getting you to draw something. To start, press the big play button on the home screen. Then input the amount of time you want to spend per drawing, and the amount of drawings you want to do before clicking continue.
You will then be taken to a page with a drawing canvas, a timer, and a few buttons. The buttons represent various different functions of the drawing tool, you can change your stroke size, color, switch to eraser, undo or redo the last action, or skip to the next drawing. Make sure you pay attention to the clock and draw the topic word before time runs out!
Once you are done drawing the first round, you will be asked if you want to continue. If you click yes you will go back around and draw again, if you click no you will be taken to the end screen and shown your final drawings. Have fun drawing!
